# Service Standards

Exceptional service is what sets Kaleo Hospitality apart. These standards define how we treat every guest, every time.

## The Guest Journey

<Steps>
  <Step title="Welcome (within 30 seconds)">
    Greet every guest with eye contact, a genuine smile, and a warm welcome. Use the guest's name if you have it from a reservation.
    > "Welcome to Kaleo — we're so glad you're here."
  </Step>
  <Step title="Seat & Present">
    Escort guests to their table. Present menus, explain any specials or 86'd items, and introduce yourself.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Take the Order">
    Give guests time to settle before approaching. Repeat orders back to confirm accuracy. Note any dietary restrictions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check Back">
    Check in within 2 minutes of food delivery. Ask a specific question rather than "Is everything okay?" — e.g., "How is the salmon?"
  </Step>
  <Step title="The Farewell">
    Thank guests sincerely. Walk them toward the exit if possible. Invite them back.
    > "It was wonderful having you — we hope to see you again soon."
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Service Timing Standards

| Stage | Target Time |
|-------|------------|
| Initial greeting | Within 30 seconds of seating |
| Beverage order taken | Within 3 minutes |
| Beverages delivered | Within 5 minutes of ordering |
| Food order taken | Within 5 minutes of menu presentation |
| Appetizers delivered | Within 10–12 minutes |
| Entrees delivered | Within 20–25 minutes |
| Check presented (when requested) | Within 3 minutes |

## Handling Complaints

1. **Listen** — Let the guest finish without interrupting.
2. **Apologize sincerely** — Not defensively. "I'm so sorry about that."
3. **Act** — Offer a solution. Replace the dish, offer a comp, or get a manager.
4. **Follow up** — Return to the table after the solution to make sure they're satisfied.
5. **Log it** — All complaints must be logged in the guest feedback sheet.

<Warning>
  Never argue with a guest. If a situation is escalating, thank them for their patience and get a manager immediately.
</Warning>
