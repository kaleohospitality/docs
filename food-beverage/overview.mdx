## Core F&B Principles

- **Quality first** — Never send out food that you wouldn't be proud to serve.
- **Consistency** — Every dish should look and taste the same regardless of who made it.
- **Safety** — Food safety is non-negotiable. When in doubt, throw it out.

<Warning>
  Allergen requests must be treated with the utmost seriousness. See the Allergen Policy page for the full protocol.
</Warning>
