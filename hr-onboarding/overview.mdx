## Hiring Philosophy

We hire for attitude and train for skill. Every new team member should feel welcomed, prepared, and set up to succeed from day one.

<Tip>
  Managers: send new hires a welcome message and a link to this knowledge base before their first shift so they can review policies and expectations ahead of time.
</Tip>
