# Employee Handbook

This handbook outlines the expectations, policies, and culture of Kaleo Hospitality. All team members are expected to read and follow these guidelines.

## Our Mission

To create exceptional guest experiences at every Kaleo Hospitality location — with the energy, consistency, and care that makes our restaurants stand out.

## Code of Conduct

Every team member is expected to:

- Treat guests and colleagues with respect and professionalism at all times
- Arrive on time and in proper uniform for every shift
- Follow all health, safety, and food handling regulations
- Protect the confidentiality of guest information
- Report any unsafe conditions, harassment, or misconduct immediately

## Attendance & Scheduling

- Schedules are posted **one week in advance**
- Shift swap requests must be approved by a manager **48 hours in advance**
- Calling out same-day requires a manager call (not a text) at least **2 hours before your shift**
- Three unexcused absences in a 90-day period may result in disciplinary action

## Uniform Standards

- Wear the designated Kaleo Hospitality uniform, clean and wrinkle-free
- Closed-toe, non-slip shoes are required in all kitchen and service areas
- Minimal jewelry; no excessive fragrances
- Hair must be tied back in food preparation areas

## Tools You'll Use

- **Jolt** — Your daily checklist tool. All opening, closing, and shift checklists are completed here on the store tablet. You'll also receive tasks and announcements through Jolt.
- **Zenput** — Temperature monitoring for our walk-in units and food items. Managers use this to track compliance across locations.
- **POS System** — Order entry, cash handling, and end-of-day reporting.

## Compensation & Benefits

- Pay periods are bi-weekly. Direct deposit is strongly encouraged.
- Pay stubs are available through your manager or the payroll portal.
- PTO eligibility and accrual details are provided at your 90-day review.

## Performance Reviews

Team members receive a formal review at 90 days and annually thereafter. Reviews cover attendance, guest feedback, skill development, and teamwork.

## Disciplinary Policy

Issues are addressed using a progressive discipline process: verbal warning → written warning → final warning → termination. Serious violations (theft, harassment, unsafe behavior) may result in immediate termination.
