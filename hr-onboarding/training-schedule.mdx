# Training Schedule

All new team members follow a structured training program. The timeline below is a guide — managers should adapt based on the individual's pace and role.

## Week-by-Week Overview

| Week | Focus | Goal |
|------|-------|------|
| Week 1 | Orientation & Observation | Understand the property, team, and culture |
| Week 2 | Systems & Skills | POS, procedures, and role-specific skills |
| Week 3 | Supervised Practice | Independent work with manager feedback |
| Week 4 | Evaluation & Sign-Off | Demonstrate competency across all core areas |

## By Role

### Front of House (Server / Host / Bartender)

<Steps>
  <Step title="Days 1–2: Orientation">
    Property tour, handbook review, system logins, and menu familiarization.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Days 3–5: Shadow shifts">
    Shadow experienced team members across all dayparts.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Week 2: Guided practice">
    Handle sections/stations with a trainer present for support.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Week 3–4: Solo with check-ins">
    Work independently; daily debrief with trainer or manager.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Back of House (Line Cook / Prep)

<Steps>
  <Step title="Days 1–2: Safety & sanitation">
    Food safety certification review, kitchen layout, equipment training.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Days 3–5: Station observation">
    Shadow each line station during service.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Week 2: Prep training">
    Complete prep lists under supervision; learn mise en place standards.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Week 3–4: Line work">
    Assigned to a station with the sous chef or lead cook on standby.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Training Sign-Off Criteria

Before a team member is considered fully trained, they must demonstrate:

- [ ] Proficiency in the POS / kitchen display system
- [ ] Knowledge of the full menu including allergens
- [ ] Ability to execute opening or closing procedures independently
- [ ] Completion of all required compliance training
- [ ] Manager sign-off on final evaluation form

<Info>
  Completed training sign-off forms go in the employee file. The team member keeps a copy.
</Info>
