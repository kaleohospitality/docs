# Daily Checklists

All daily checklists are managed in **Jolt**. Team members complete checklists on the store tablet. Managers review completion status from the Jolt dashboard — overdue or missed checklists are flagged automatically.

<Info>
  To access checklists: open the Jolt app on the store tablet → select your location → tap the checklist assigned to your shift. Edits to checklist templates are done on the **Jolt website** (not the app).
</Info>

## Temperature Monitoring — Every Shift

Temperatures are logged in Jolt using a combination of **Zenput sensors** (walk-ins) and **Bluetooth thermometer** (food items).

| Item | Tool | Required Range | Frequency |
|------|------|----------------|-----------|
| Walk-in Freezer | Zenput sensor | 0°F or below | AM / PM / Close |
| Walk-in Cooler | Zenput sensor | 34–40°F | AM / PM / Close |
| Milk / custard mix | Bluetooth thermometer | 34–40°F | AM / PM |
| Strawberries | Bluetooth thermometer | 34–40°F | AM / PM |
| Chili (hot hold) | Bluetooth thermometer | 135°F+ | AM / PM |
| Cheese slices | Bluetooth thermometer | 34–40°F | AM / PM |
| Sanitizer (all stations) | Test strip (enter number) | Per chemical spec | AM / PM / Close |

<Warning>
  If a temperature is out of range, **stop using that product** and notify the manager immediately. Document in Jolt before taking any corrective action. Do not override or guess — log it accurately.
</Warning>

## How Temperature Questions Work in Jolt

- **Sensor temperature questions** (walk-in freezer, walk-in cooler): Select the sensor placement from the list — the app auto-populates the reading from the Zenput sensor.
- **Bluetooth thermometer questions** (food items): Pair the Bluetooth thermometer with the tablet, then take the temp — it populates automatically.
- **Number questions** (chemicals): Enter the reading manually from the test strip.

If a sensor isn't showing up or the Bluetooth connection drops, note it in Jolt and take a manual reading with the backup thermometer. Contact the manager — do not skip the question.
