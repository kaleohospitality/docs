# New Hire Checklist

Welcome to Kaleo Hospitality! Use this checklist to make sure you're set up and ready to go.

## Before Day One (Manager)

- [ ] Send offer letter and confirm start date
- [ ] Collect completed I-9 and W-4 documents
- [ ] Add employee to scheduling and payroll systems
- [ ] Assign a buddy/mentor for the first week
- [ ] Prepare uniform, name badge, and any access credentials

## Day One

<Steps>
  <Step title="Welcome & tour">
    Manager gives a property tour, introduces the team, and reviews the day's agenda.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Paperwork & systems">
    Complete any remaining HR paperwork. Set up POS login, clocking system, and any required apps.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review handbook">
    Read through the Employee Handbook together. Answer any questions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Shadow a senior team member">
    Observe a full shift with an experienced colleague in the same role.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## First Week

- [ ] Complete all required compliance training (food safety, harassment prevention)
- [ ] Learn POS system and practice common transactions
- [ ] Review menu and F&B standards with team lead
- [ ] Complete at least 2 observed and evaluated shifts
- [ ] Meet with manager for a check-in at the end of week one

## Documents to Collect

| Document | Due |
|----------|-----|
| Signed Employee Handbook | Day 1 |
| Food Handler Certification | Within 30 days |
| Emergency contact form | Day 1 |
| Direct deposit form | Day 1 |

<Info>
  All signed documents should be submitted to the manager and filed in the employee folder. Digital copies go to HR.
</Info>
