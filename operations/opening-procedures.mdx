# Opening Procedures

Complete all steps in order before the property opens to guests. Initial each item on the physical log sheet.

## Arrival & Security

<Steps>
  <Step title="Arrive on time">
    Arrive at least 15 minutes before your shift starts. Clock in using the designated system.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Secure the premises">
    Check all entry points are secure. Disable the alarm using your personal code.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Conduct a walkthrough">
    Walk all guest-facing areas and note any maintenance issues, cleanliness concerns, or safety hazards.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Front of House Setup

- [ ] Turn on all lighting to the correct levels per area
- [ ] Check that all tables/seating areas are clean and properly set
- [ ] Confirm reservation system is active and today's bookings are loaded
- [ ] Stock host stand (menus, sanitizer, pens)
- [ ] Verify music/ambient settings are appropriate

## Back of House Setup

- [ ] Confirm all equipment is powered on and at proper temperatures
- [ ] Review prep lists with kitchen team
- [ ] Check inventory against opening par levels
- [ ] Ensure all team members are in proper uniform

## Communication

- [ ] Review any notes from the previous shift's closing log
- [ ] Brief the team on any specials, 86'd items, or VIP guests
- [ ] Confirm all staff positions are covered for the shift

<Tip>
  If any critical issue is found during the walkthrough (e.g., broken equipment, safety hazard), notify your manager immediately before proceeding.
</Tip>
