## 🎉 New: Kaleo Hospitality Knowledge Base

We're excited to launch the Kaleo Hospitality Knowledge Base — your team's central hub for everything you need to deliver exceptional hospitality. Browse by section in the sidebar or use search to jump straight to what you need.

### New features

- **Employee Handbook (Rev 2026.02).** The full, current handbook is now live with policies on employment, attendance and conduct, dress code, technology, anti-discrimination and harassment, pay and benefits, safety, and more. See the [Employee Handbook](/hr-onboarding/employee-handbook).
- **Operations & Procedures.** Step-by-step [opening procedures](/operations/opening-procedures), [closing procedures](/operations/closing-procedures), and [daily checklists](/operations/daily-checklists) for consistent day-to-day execution. Start at the [Operations overview](/operations/overview).
- **HR & Onboarding.** A [new hire checklist](/hr-onboarding/new-hire-checklist) and [training schedule](/hr-onboarding/training-schedule) to help managers and new team members hit the ground running. Start at the [HR & Onboarding overview](/hr-onboarding/overview).
- **Food & Beverage standards.** Reference materials for [menu standards](/food-beverage/menu-standards), [service standards](/food-beverage/service-standards), and our [allergen policy](/food-beverage/allergen-policy). Start at the [Food & Beverage overview](/food-beverage/overview).
- **Search.** Find any policy, procedure, or standard from the search bar at the top of every page.

### What this means for you

- **New team members** have one place to onboard, review policies, and learn the standards.
- **Managers** can point team members to a single source of truth instead of tracking down PDFs and emails.
- **All team members** can quickly look up procedures, allergen guidance, and service expectations on shift.
