# Opening Procedures

Complete all steps in order before opening to guests. The opening manager is responsible for completing the **Jolt Opening Checklist** and ensuring it's submitted before the first guest enters.

<Info>
  All opening tasks are tracked in **Jolt**. Log in to the store tablet, select your location, and complete the Opening Checklist. Missed or overdue checklists are flagged to management automatically.
</Info>

## Arrival & Security

<Steps>
  <Step title="Manager arrives 30–45 minutes early">
    The opening manager arrives first to unlock the building and disable the alarm using their personal code. Clock in via the POS system.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Walk the building">
    Walk all areas — dining room, kitchen, drive-thru, restrooms. Note any maintenance issues, safety hazards, or cleanliness concerns from the prior close. Log issues in Jolt.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Equipment check">
    Confirm all refrigeration is holding temperature. Check Zenput sensor readings for the **walk-in freezer** and **walk-in cooler** before any food handling begins. If either is out of range, notify Chad and the service line before opening.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Temperature & Food Safety

Complete the **Jolt Temperature Log** at opening. These are mandatory — do not skip.

| Item | Method | Safe Range |
|------|--------|------------|
| Walk-in Freezer | Zenput sensor | 0°F or below |
| Walk-in Cooler | Zenput sensor | 34–40°F |
| Custard mix / milk | Bluetooth thermometer | 34–40°F |
| Chili | Bluetooth thermometer | 135°F+ (hot hold) |
| Strawberries | Bluetooth thermometer | 34–40°F |
| Cheese slices | Bluetooth thermometer | 34–40°F |
| Sanitizer concentration | Manual test strip | Per chemical spec |

<Warning>
  If any food temperature is out of safe range at opening, **do not use that product**. Notify the manager immediately and document in Jolt before discarding.
</Warning>

## Kitchen Setup

- [ ] Turn on all equipment — grills, fryers, custard machines — and allow proper warm-up time
- [ ] Verify steam table and hot holding equipment is at temperature
- [ ] Pull and date-check all prepped items from the cooler
- [ ] Complete morning prep list before service begins
- [ ] Confirm all team members are in uniform with name tags

## Front of House Setup

- [ ] Unlock dining room and check tables and chairs for cleanliness
- [ ] Restock condiment stations, napkin dispensers, and straws
- [ ] Confirm drive-thru headsets are charged and functioning
- [ ] Check that the POS system is online and the day's shift is open
- [ ] Spot-clean windows, doors, and any visible surfaces

## Pre-Open Communication

- [ ] Review the prior shift's closing notes for any carry-over issues
- [ ] Confirm staffing is complete — fill open slots before opening if possible
- [ ] Brief the team on any 86'd items, product issues, or special ops notes for the day

<Tip>
  The Jolt Opening Checklist must be **fully submitted** before unlocking the doors. If you're running short on time, prioritize food safety temps first — never skip those.
</Tip>
