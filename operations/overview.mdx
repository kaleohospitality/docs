## Guiding Principles

1. **Consistency** — Every guest should receive the same high standard of service every visit.
2. **Accountability** — Team members are responsible for completing their assigned checklists each shift.
3. **Communication** — Shift handoffs should include a verbal and written update on any open issues.

<Warning>
  All operational incidents (spills, accidents, complaints) must be logged in the incident log the same day they occur.
</Warning>
